This is a demonatration of the parametric line segment equation:
  • p = v1 + t * (v2 - v1)
  • p is a point, drawn in blue
  • v1 and v2 are the two line segment endpoints
You can
  • drag the two black points, v1 and v2, and
  • move the mouse to the left and right to alter the value of t.
