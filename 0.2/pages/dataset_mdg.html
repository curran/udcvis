UN Millenium Development Goals Indicators

Here is a quote typical of the MDG effort, from their 2012 Annual Report:

  • Extreme poverty is falling in every region
    For the first time since poverty trends began to be monitored, the number of people living in extreme poverty and poverty rates fell in every developing region - including in subSaharan Africa, where rates are highest. The proportion of people living on less than $1.25 a day fell from 47 per cent in 1990 to 24 per cent in 2008 - a reduction from over 2 billion to less than 1.4 billion.
